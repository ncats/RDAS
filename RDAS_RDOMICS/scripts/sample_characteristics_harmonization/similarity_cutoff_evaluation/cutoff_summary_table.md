Similarity cutoff evaluation summary

Candidate assignments were evaluated for labels remaining after rule-based matching (n = 1,378 labels). Manual accuracy was calculated as the proportion of reviewed assignments judged correct within the reviewed subset.

| Similarity cutoff | Labels assigned | Label coverage among remaining labels | Occurrences assigned | Occurrence coverage among remaining occurrences | Reviewed labels | Reviewed correct | Manual accuracy |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 0.70 | 534 | 38.8% | 198,809 | 55.2% | 150 | 67 | 44.7% |
| 0.75 | 333 | 24.2% | 146,907 | 40.8% | 100 | 45 | 45.0% |
| 0.80 | 163 | 11.8% | 42,280 | 11.7% | 50 | 24 | 48.0% |

The 0.75 cutoff was selected for the final pipeline because it provided substantially greater label coverage than 0.80 while maintaining similar manual accuracy.
